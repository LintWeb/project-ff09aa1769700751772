About the Competition

The Cricket Championship 2025 is an annual tournament featuring the best cricket teams from around the region. This year, we have eight teams competing in a round-robin format followed by playoffs.

The tournament emphasizes fair play, sportsmanship, and the spirit of cricket. Matches are played on weekends, with finals scheduled for the end of the season.
